UC3M Econometric Group

We are very happy to share with you the details of the forthcoming Workshop on Modelling Economic and Financial Time Series.

The UC3M Econometric Group is a forum based at the Department of Statistics at the Carlos III University of Madrid for the exchange and dissemination of research work in econometrics. It will be held at , Carlos III University of Madrid and is aimed at academics and PhD students.

If you would like to attend this event please register through the link. We welcome you to present in our post session.Please submit abstracts for the workshop under the theme “Modelling Economic and Financial Time Series” through abstract submission.
